A Poem to The Flowers

Hi, I am Olga. Welcome to the website with my works made in ancient technique of ribbon embroidery. I made them with all my love and passion to flowers and I hope you will enjoy them. I gathered all of them in my Gallery in a little bit chaotic way, but the filters can be helpful. In the section Videos you can find videos and other stuff about ribbon embroidery.

I do not sell my works. This website is only for fun.

Do you like roses, pansies and other flowers? Then visit the gallery with my works.
About Ribbon Embroidery

Ribbon embroidery is a delicate and intricate art form that has adorned fabrics for centuries. Its origins can be traced back to ancient civilizations, where it began as a simple technique for decorating clothing and has since evolved into a sophisticated craft celebrated for its beauty and elegance.
